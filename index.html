Our Mission

First Gen Foundry was founded by Hanna Sha in 2026 to bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity. We believe that every female first-generation, low-income student deserves access to the tools, connections, and support needed to thrive in their careers—regardless of background. Hanna understands the challenges and opportunities that first-gen students face.

We provide mentorship, workshops, and hands-on support in the areas that matter most: professional development, internship placement, networking skills, and resume building. Together, we're creating a community where first-gen women can succeed.

What We Offer

Professional Development

Workshops and training in leadership, communication, confidence-building, and career strategy—built for first-gen women.

Internship Searches

Guided support in finding, applying to, and securing internships that align with your goals and open doors.

Networking

Connections with mentors, peers, and professionals. Learn how to build and nurture a network that lifts you up.

Resume & Career Materials

One-on-one resume reviews, cover letter support, and guidance on crafting materials that get you noticed.

Our Impact

First Gen Foundry is building a community of resilient, supported women ready to lead. Every student we support takes steps toward economic mobility and professional success.
